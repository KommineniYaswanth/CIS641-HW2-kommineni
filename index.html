Project Proposal: Hydration Reminder App

Team: Data Wizards

Team Members: Likhithasree Kommineni, Yaswanth Naga Babu Kommineni, Srinija Reddy Kotla

The project's goal is to develop a cutting-edge smartphone application that assists users in staying hydrated. Users can establish customizable goals for water intake, track their daily consumption, and receive frequent push reminders throughout the day via an easy-to-use interface.

The app will provide users with a comprehensive picture of their hydration patterns by integrating seamlessly with popular health-tracking programs and smartwatches. It will utilize data analytics to offer tailored recommendations, enhancing users' overall well-being by encouraging them to lead healthier lifestyles.

Technologies Used:

  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript for front-end development
  • Python for back-end functionality
  • GitHub for version control
  • MongoDB for data storage

Estimated Timeline:

Week 1-2: Research and design.
Week 3-4: Front-end development.
Week 5-6: Back-end development.
Week 6-7: Testing and debugging.
Week 7-8: Deployment and feedback gathering.

Anticipated Challenges:

Ensuring timely notifications and securing user data, particularly personal health information.
